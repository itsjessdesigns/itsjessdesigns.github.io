FIVE SUMMER SAUCES

Meal prep made easy with these 5 versatile simmer sauces that bring bold, comforting flavors to your weeknight dinners! Whether you're tossing pasta in the creamy Alfredo or Spicy "Vodka" Sauce, adding chicken and veggies to the Thai Red Curry or Butter Chicken Sauce, or baking up cheesy enchiladas with the Green Enchilada Sauce, dinner is done in no time. Stock your fridge or freezer with these homemade sauces to make mealtime stress-free and delicious!

PREP TIME: 15 MINUTES

COOK TIME: 1 HOUR 15 MINUTES

TOTAL TIME: 1 HOUR 30 MINUTES

INGREDIENTS

BUTTER CHICKEN

  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • 2 teaspoons ginger paste
  • 1 tablespoon fresh garlic, minced or paste
  • 2 tablespoons garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon coriander
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 to 2 teaspoons cayenne powder, to taste for spiciness
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon tumeric
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon sugar, or sweetness to taste

INSTRUCTIONS

MAKE THE BUTTER CHICKEN SAUCE

  1. Heat ghee or oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Saut√© the ginger and garlic paste until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Add garam masala, coriander, cumin, cayenne powder, salt, and turmeric. Stir well to toast the spices for another minute.
  4. Stir in tomato paste and tomato sauce, cooking until the mixture thickens slightly.
  5. Reduce the heat to low, then stir in heavy cream, butter, and sugar. Simmer gently for 5-7 minutes until the sauce is rich and creamy.
