The Big Reveal

If you haven't noticed by now, the first 3 covers are cropped in a strage way, some of the words get cut of but there is always at least one word visible. This was on purpose from the very beginning of Zerobaseone's carrer. If you look, the first album says YOU HAD, the second says ME and the pre-release single cuts off the word SWEAT to say AT. Shortly after the single was released the full message was revealed in the thrid albums title YOU HAD ME AT HELLO.
